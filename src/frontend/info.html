üéÆ How to Play

1. Create an Account

Click the Login or Register button to create your account. Just pick any username and password - it's that simple! Two users can't have the same username, but that's the only rule.

2. Select Your Color

Choose from 11 vibrant preset colors or use the custom color picker (the rainbow square) to select any color you want. Your selected color appears in the preview box below the palette.

3. Place Your Pixels

  • Desktop: Click on any cell in the 64√ó64 grid to place a pixel
  • Mobile: Tap on any cell to place your pixel
  • You can place one pixel every 3 seconds
  • Watch the timer in the canvas toolbar to see when you can place your next pixel

4. Navigate the Canvas

  • Zoom In/Out: Use the + and - buttons or scroll with your mouse wheel
  • Pan: Click and drag to move around the canvas (or use touch gestures on mobile)
  • Reset View: Click the ‚ü≤ button to return to the center
  • Mobile: Pinch to zoom, swipe to pan
